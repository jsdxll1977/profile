Biography

Associate Professor and Master's Supervisor, earned a Ph.D. in Computer Application Technology in 2020. Long engaged in research on IoT, cloud computing, and related technologies, participated in multiple NSFC projects, published over ten papers, and filed two patents. Primarily teaches courses including Java Web, Requirements Engineering, Software Testing, Data Structures, and Operating Systems. Led or participated in numerous IoT and software development projects, with extensive experience in IoT system architecture, Android software design, and J2EE project architecture.
